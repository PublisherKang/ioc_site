IOC GENESIS LIMITED EDITION MINT

An exclusive very limited series of NFT artworks and Decentraland wearables....
IOC GENESIS is the first digital collection for the Metaverse from Imitation of Christ. It is an extremely limited mint with only 1000 units. Co-created by visionary artists Tara Subkoff and Adam Teninbaum, in this collection you will find a number of unique designs, including collaborations with Lynsey Addario, Nicole Buffett, and 2 BAYC NFTs. This Limited Edition Collection sets the tone for the future of IOC-NFT in the Metaverse and unlocks the value of exclusive access. IOC GENESIS NFTs will serve as Allow list entries for future IOC-NFT collections. Owners will also be invited to exclusive shows and events, and have the ability to preview looks well in advance of their release to the public. The collection will live on the Polygon Blockchain.

The team is donating a portion of the profits to the UKRAINEDAO, to support the people of Ukraine and shed light upon and oppose the atrocities of Putin’s war.
